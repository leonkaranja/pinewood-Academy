Welcome to Pinewood Academy, where excellence in education, personal growth, and community engagement are at the heart of everything we do. For over 28 years, Pinewood has been dedicated to providing a transformative educational experience for students of all backgrounds. Our school has earned a reputation for academic rigor, outstanding extracurricular programs, and a commitment to shaping future leaders.

Pinewood was founded in 1996 by Leon Karanja, with a vision to provide a well-rounded education that prepares students to excel academically and socially. In its early years, Pinewood focused on creating a strong academic foundation for students, emphasizing critical thinking and creativity. Over the years, our school has expanded both in size and reputation, continually adapting to meet the needs of our diverse student body and the evolving demands of the modern world. We are proud of our rich history of academic excellence, community involvement, and commitment to fostering lifelong learners. From our humble beginnings, Pinewood has always prioritized the success and well-being of our students. Today, we continue to uphold the values that have guided us from the start while embracing new opportunities for growth and innovation.

At Pinewood, we are committed to fostering a learning environment rooted in strong ethical principles and a commitment to excellence. Our core values guide every aspect of our academic and social endeavors:

Integrity: We believe in fostering honesty, responsibility, and accountability in all of our students. Integrity shapes the way we interact with one another, guiding us to make ethical choices both inside and outside the classroom.

Respect: We value diversity and cultivate an atmosphere where every individual is treated with dignity. Respect for others, the environment, and our community is foundational to our school culture.

Excellence: We strive for academic and personal excellence in everything we do. We challenge our students to go beyond their limits and achieve their highest potential, encouraging them to take pride in their work and contributions.

Collaboration: Learning is a community effort. We encourage teamwork, open communication, and the sharing of ideas, enabling our students to learn not only from teachers but from each other.

Innovation: We foster creativity and problem-solving skills that prepare students for the future. Our students are empowered to think critically and approach challenges with an open, innovative mindset.


Awards and Achievements

Pinewood is proud to be recognized for excellence in academics, STEM, the arts, community service, and athletics. Our students consistently earn top honors, showcasing their dedication and our commitment to providing a well-rounded education.
